## FinPocket 
Managing your money is hard, and even tracking them is even more hard, FinPocket introducing web app, where you are managing and tracking your monthy income or amount.

So that you can make better decision and get to know where is your money is going , you can see all of those in dashboard, where are multiple charts, that show different types of category of spending.

User can even set a amount of saving amount so that on spending on a particular amount user will get notification of his saving goal is in danger. 

User can also set reminder, if can be your rent, or your purchasing goal.

And the best is your data is stored in safe hand, and you can montior your previous month spending or even year spending on history section.
